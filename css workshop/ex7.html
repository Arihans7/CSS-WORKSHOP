Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup of strawberries (optional)

Instructions

In a blender, combine milk, ice cream, and sugar. Blend until smooth. If using strawberries, add them and blend again until well mixed. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.
